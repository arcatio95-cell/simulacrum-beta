Welcome to SIMulacrum
Your real life, gamified. Every habit you build earns XP across 7 core attributes — just like a D&D character sheet, but the character is you.
📊
7 Attributes
Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom, Intelligence, Charisma, Wealth — each levels from 1 to 20.
✅
Daily Habits → XP
Check off your daily tasks to earn up to 100 XP per attribute. Consistent effort compounds into real levels.

Streak System
Consecutive days of logging build a streak. Hit 7, 30, and 100-day milestones. Earn shields to protect your streak.

Challenges & Titles
